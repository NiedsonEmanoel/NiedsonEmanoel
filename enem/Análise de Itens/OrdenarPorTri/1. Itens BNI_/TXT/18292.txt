The Four Oxen and the Lion

A Lion used to prowi about a field in which Four
Oxen used to live. Many a time he tried to attack them;
but whenever he came near, they tumed their tails to one
another, so that whichever way he approached them he
was met by the homs of one of them. At last, however,
they quarreled among themselves, and each went off to
pasture alone in a separate comer of the field. Then the
Lion attacked them one by one and soon made an end of
all four.
